About Me

Hello, I'm a passionate and motivated full stack developer. I recently (April 2023) graduated with a Master's in Computer Science from the Univeristy of Waterloo. With a strong educational background and a deep fascination for technology, I am dedicated to creating innovative and efficient web applications.

An anecdote about me:
I like to award myself with the epithet "The Accidental Engineer", with a self-deprecating undertone. Using my school teachers as a crutch to complete a final year visual basic billing application, choosing CS as a career path through a google search of "top-10 trendy fields" and being unable to compile a "Hello World" C program before the start of freshman year, are among the few reasons that lead to the aforementioned epithet.
